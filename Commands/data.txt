autoplay|Automatically plays songs without stopping.
clearqueue|Clears all songs from the queue.
disconnect|Disconnects the bot from the voice channel.
join|Connects the bot to the voice channel.
loop|Toggles loop on or off.
nowplaying|Displays the currently playing song.
pause|Pauses the current song.
play|Plays a song by name or URL.
queue|Displays the list of songs in the queue.
remove|Removes a song from the queue.
seek|Jumps to a specific time in the song.
skip|Skips the current song.
skipto|Skips directly to a specific song in the queue.
volume|Changes the playback volume.
